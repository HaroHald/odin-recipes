Spaghetti Bolognese

Everyone needs a great everyday Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, and this is mine!

The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour. It is perfect for a quick midweek meal but even better if you can simmer it for a couple of hours! Serve it over pasta, stuff into jacket potatoes, make an epic Lasagna or Baked Spaghetti Pie!

Here I will teach you how to make a delishious dish like this one, tell you what you need and give a step by step explanation on making this tasty dish.

Ingredients:

  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Beef bouillon cubes
  • Beef
  • Sugar
  • Thyme
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Red wine
  • Bay leaves

Steps in making the Spaghetti:

  1. Saute garlic and onion for about 3 minutes;
  2. Brown the beef about 2 minutes;
  3. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum (midweek), up to 3 hours (weekend indulgence!). Slow cooking makes the beef incredibly tender and the sauce develops extra flavour.
